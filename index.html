BMW M4 Competition

The BMW M4 Competition is a four-seater coupe with a 2993 cc, 6-cylinder, inline, petrol engine. It has a top speed of 250 kmph and can accelerate from 0–100 kmph in 3.5 seconds. The M4 Competition has a ground clearance of 120 mm and comes with six airbags and an NCAP rating of 5 stars. It has an ARAI mileage of 9.7 kmpl.
