BMW M5 CS

The BMW M5 CS is a high-performance, four-door sedan that's the top variant in the 5 Series sedan line. The CS stands for "Competition Sport" and is a designation for exclusive BMW M GmbH models that are suitable for racetracks. The 2022 BMW M5 CS is the fastest sedan currently offered in production by BMW.
